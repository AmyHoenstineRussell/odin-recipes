Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • ground beef
  • noodles
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Saute the ground beef so that it is cooked through.
  2. Add a layer of tomato sauce to the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Add a layer of lasagne noodles on top of the sauce layer.
  4. Add a layer of ground beef, a layer of cheese, and a layer of sauce.
  5. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until your baking dish is filled.
  6. Bake at 375F for 50 minutes.
